Our Dream

We think big but we dream bigger

We believe that business should be exciting and collaborative - not terrifying and secluded. Our dream is that we empower businesses to see their maximum growth potential. Many businesses, regardless of industry, face the same core problems. Our mentors take pride in helping to grow companies. Mentors are the true "behind the scenes CEO".

OUR MENTORS

Founding member Brandon Kruse has significant experience building and cultivating successful companies. From telecom and IT to real estate and finance - many small businesses face the same challenges over and over that can be easily avoided with expertise and guidance. Brandon and his team, are available for expertise requests and general know-how. Part of being in this community is the overwhelming support of it's mentors - who will help guide in strategic business administration and technology (email/web hosting and design/servers/telecom/product development).
