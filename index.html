LEARN FROM ENTREPRENEURS

Our goal at the Huntsville Entrepreneurial Initiative incubator is to help support the culture and development of small businesses. We bring together some of the most successful business icons in our community to help mentor and foster small businesses. We believe entrepreneurial education is lacking in our community. We aim to change this through our incubation program and small business community

OUR MENTORS

Founding members Brandon Kruse and Glenn Clayton have both had significant experience building and cultivating successful companies. From education to telecom and IT - many small businesses face the same challenges over and over that can be easily avoided with expertise and guidance. Both Brandon and Glenn, as well as their teams, are available for expertise requests and general know-how. Part of being in this community is the overwhelming support of it's mentors - who will help guide in strategic business administration and technology (email/web hosting and design/servers/telecom/product development).
